## Slope
$(\frac{M}{P})^s=L(r,Y)$
$0=\frac{\delta L}{\delta r}\frac{\delta r}{\delta Y}+\frac{\delta L}{\delta Y}$
$\frac{\delta r}{\delta Y}=-\frac{\delta L}{\delta Y}/\frac{\delta L}{\delta r}$
The slope is greater than zero as the slope of L wrt. r is negative
